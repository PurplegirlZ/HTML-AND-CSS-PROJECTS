Chocolate

Chocolate is alot of peoples favorite, it taste good, and smells better. People have put chocolate on everything. I've even seen chocolate covered bacon before.

Mint candy

Mint is enjoyable in candy canes and in gum. Alot of people like to pop a mint to help quickly get their breath smelling clean but also because they may enjoy the cool feeling that come when eating mint candy.

Gummy candy

Gummy candy can be really fun. These come in all sorts of shapes and tastes. I personally love gummy sushi and pizza, but a favorite for alot of people everywhere are gummy worms.

Hard candy

Like lollipops, jolly ranchers, and any other candy you'll just sit there and suck on. These candies are nice to just sit back with. Jaw breakers are really hard candy too but thankfully they most likely won't actually break your jaw.

Sour candy

Sour candy is not most peoples go to unless they are doing some sort of chalenge. It can cause your face to pucker and you to sit there wondering if it was worth it. I personally like sour candy in little burst but to much can leave my tongue hurting.
