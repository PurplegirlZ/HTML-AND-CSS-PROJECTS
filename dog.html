Dalmation puppies

You can find 99 puppies hidden in chest in all the worlds up to Hollow Bastion, except for Atlantis. Each chest has 3 puppies in them and each world has mulitple chest of puppies. Getting all the puppies can also unlock the secret endings, which there are 2 of, and it upgrades you Aero spell which you use as a sheild of enemies get to close. You also get the gift of all the puppies being back home safe and sound with Pongo and Perdita.

There are 4 chest with puppies in it in every world except for 2 which are Olympus Coliseum which only have 1 chest, and Atlantis. This comic here will explain exactly why you will not find them in Atlantis...

After finding about 15 puppies you will get a gift from Pongo and Perdy, there are many different gifts you can get from these dogs which can be pretty good for you. The chest to find them can be in tricky spots and hard to locate so here is a video explaining how to find them all.
