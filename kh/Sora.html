Sora

Sora is the one you play as in most of the Kingdom Hearts games. He is a kind young boy who just generally wants to help people.
Sora's main weapon is a keyblade is the only keyblade wielder who did not get a ceremony. It turns out his keyblade was originally going to be Riku's keyblade but when Riku disappeared Sora got it instead.
Funfact: Sora originally was going to be half lion with a chainsaw keyblade, but they chose the design we have now which I like better.
